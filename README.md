0. Introduction  
Built the Chiliz (CHZ) Staking Dashboard app (the "App") as a free utility for the Chiliz community. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.  
Take full control of your rewards in the Chiliz ecosystem with the ultimate companion app for CHZ holders. Designed specifically for the Chiliz Chain, this dashboard offers a simple, native, and extremely fast way to track your staked assets, analyze your historical yield, and seamlessly interact with Web3 dApps.  
This page is used to inform users regarding my policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information.  
By choosing to use the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The App is designed with strict privacy and security principles: it is non-custodial, does not require private keys, and does not track personal user data.  

2. Information Collection and Use  
The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) with me or any third-party servers.  
- Public Blockchain Data: To function, the App requires you to input a public Chiliz (CHZ) wallet address (starting with 0x...). This public address is used strictly to query public blockchain ledgers to display your balances, rewards, and staking metrics.  
- Local Storage: The public wallet addresses you add, along with your preferred fiat currency setting (USD/EUR), are saved locally on your device using Android's native SharedPreferences. I do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to my servers. You can delete this data at any time by removing the wallet from the App or clearing the App's data in your device settings.  
- No Private Keys: The App will never ask for, collect, or have access to your private keys, seed phrases, or passwords.  

3. Third-Party Services and APIs  
To provide accurate live data, the App communicates directly with the following third-party public APIs and services. These services may log standard network request data (such as your IP address) as part of normal internet operations:  
- Chiliz Official APIs (cc-api.chiliz.com & rpc.chiliz.com) for staking metrics and network supply.  
- RouteScan / Chiliscan for historical transaction data to calculate account age.  
- CoinGecko API for live CHZ to USD/EUR fiat conversion rates.  
The "Staking" tab within the App utilizes an Android WebView to securely load the official Chiliz staking portal (https://app.chilizchain.com/staking). Your interactions within this WebView are governed by the privacy policy of the Chiliz Chain ecosystem.  

4. Web3 Wallet Interactions (Intents)  
The App includes features that allow you to interact with external Web3 wallets (such as MetaMask or Trust Wallet) installed on your device via standard Android Deep Links (EIP-681).  
- When triggering a donation or connecting a wallet via the WebView, the App simply formats the transaction request and hands it over to your device's operating system.  
- The App cannot read your external wallet's contents, intercept transactions, or authorize transfers on your behalf. All transactions must be manually reviewed and approved by you within your chosen external wallet.
